Here's the latest from the Daily Record. A Scots charity, Mary’s Meals, is now feeding three million children every day worldwide—an incredible milestone for the group, which began in a rickety tin shed in Argyll back in 2002. The founder says he “could never have imagined” reaching so many. 

Meanwhile, concerns have been raised over millions of pounds handed out in golden goodbyes to public sector officials. Critics argue that four million pounds, though a small part of the Scottish Government’s budget, could have repaired many roads and pavements.
