Here's the latest from the Daily Record. Oasis fans are flocking to Edinburgh ahead of three sold-out shows at Murrayfield, marking the Mancunian rockers’ first return to Scotland in sixteen years. The concerts are set for Friday, Saturday, and Tuesday, drawing huge crowds to the capital. Meanwhile, Oasis will entertain two hundred thousand Scottish fans on Friday, but concert-goers are being advised to keep an eye on the weather forecast as they head to the stadium.
